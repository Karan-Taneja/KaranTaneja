About Me

Hello, my name is Karan Taneja, but I go by TK. I am currently enrolled in Pursuit as a Fellow of the Full-Stack Nights & Weekends cohort. I am really looking forward to showing what I am capable of. I'm striving for excellence and believe my tenacious work ethic will allow me to succeed.

My drive comes from my desire to give my family a better life. You can expect great things of me, as that's what I will to bring to the table.

People say I'm lucky, to which I say "I make my own luck". I find joy in learning new things, and being able to share it with my peers. As such, every day I try to do so, or at least better understand something. And above all else, what I want is to make an impact every day. There are so many people whose circumstances prevent them from being able to use each day to improve their lives or themselves. I want to change that.
